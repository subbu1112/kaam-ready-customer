Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Kaam Ready ("we", "us") operates the Kaam Ready customer and worker applications, which connect customers with skilled workers (electricians, plumbers, cleaners and similar services) in Karnataka, India. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

From all users: name, phone number, email address (if you sign in by email), city, and profile photo if you choose to add one.

From customers: service address and, with your permission, your device's GPS location at the time of booking so the worker can find you; booking history; ratings and reviews you submit.

From workers: home address, skills, work experience, UPI ID for receiving payments, your live location when you accept a job, photographs of completed work you choose to upload, and — for identity verification — photographs of your Aadhaar card.

2. How we use it

We use this data to match customers with nearby workers, show locations on the map during a job, enable calls between customer and worker for an active booking, process service records and ratings, verify worker identity for customer safety, and calculate platform fees. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Aadhaar and identity data

Aadhaar card images are uploaded by workers voluntarily as part of onboarding. They are stored in a private, access-controlled storage bucket and are visible only to our verification team. They are never shown to customers or other workers, and are used solely to verify identity. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8); this will remove the images and revoke your verified badge.

4. Payments

Payments are made directly from customer to worker via UPI. We do not collect, store, or process card numbers or UPI PINs. We record the agreed job amount, the worker's UPI ID, and payment confirmation status for record-keeping and dispute resolution.

5. Location data

Location is collected only when needed: when a customer creates a booking and when a worker accepts one. We do not track your location in the background. You may deny location permission; the app will work using your typed address instead.

6. Sharing

When a booking is made, the customer sees the worker's name, photo, skill, rating, and phone number; the worker sees the customer's name, address, location, and phone number — only for that booking. We share data with our infrastructure providers (database and hosting) solely to operate the service, and with authorities if required by law.

7. Storage and security

Data is stored with Supabase (database and file storage) with row-level security so each user can only access their own records. Identity documents are in private storage. Access to administrative tools is restricted and password-protected.

8. Your rights

You may request a copy of your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at reddysaiu55@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Some records (completed transaction history) may be retained where required for legal or tax purposes.

9. Children

Kaam Ready is not intended for users under 18. Workers must be 18 or older to register.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be announced in the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
