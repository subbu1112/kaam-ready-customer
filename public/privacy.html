Privacy Policy

Kaam Ready Technologies · Karnataka, India · Last updated June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Kaam Ready app collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using Kaam Ready you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account details — your name and phone number (used for OTP sign-in and to coordinate bookings).
  • Address — service addresses you save, so a worker can reach you.
  • Location — approximate and precise location, to match nearby workers and show live arrival tracking.
  • Profile photo — optional, if you add one.
  • Booking & payment history — services booked and amounts, including the UPI transaction reference. We do not store your card or bank details; payment is completed inside your UPI app.
  • Device & diagnostics — a push-notification identifier and crash/diagnostic logs to keep the app reliable.

How we use your information

To create and manage your account, match you with verified workers, enable live tracking, process pay-after-work UPI payments, send booking notifications, provide support, prevent fraud, and meet legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers. To deliver the service, the worker assigned to your job receives the details needed to complete it (such as your name, address and contact). We use trusted service providers (hosting/database, push notifications, crash reporting) who process data only on our behalf and under contract.

Data security & retention

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We retain personal data only as long as needed for the service or as required by law, after which it is deleted.

Your rights & account deletion

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To delete your account and associated data, use Profile → Delete My Account in the app, or follow our account deletion page. Account access is disabled immediately and full erasure is completed within 30 days, except records we must legally retain.

Children

Kaam Ready is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed to children.
